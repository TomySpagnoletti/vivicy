# ISS-0006 - Evaluate a month against its budget

## Summary

Implement the Reporter's budget evaluation: given a positive integer `limitCents` for a month, report whether the month's total is within budget, equal to the limit, or over budget, plus the signed difference between the total and the limit. A month exactly equal to its limit is within budget, not over.

## Scope

Add budget evaluation to the Reporter built in ISS-0005, as a pure-JS function under `src/`.

A budget is a positive integer `limitCents` for a given month. The function evaluates a month's total (the monthly total from ISS-0005) against its budget and reports:

- whether the month is within budget, equal to the limit, or over budget;
- the signed difference between the total and the limit (total minus limit).

A month whose total exactly equals its limit is reported as within budget, not over.

## Out Of Scope

Computing the monthly total itself (ISS-0005), category breakdown, and CLI wiring. This issue does not introduce alerting, persistence, or notification.

## Verification

Unit tests in `test/` (node:test) to full branch coverage: a month under its limit reports within budget with a negative signed difference; a month exactly at its limit reports within budget (not over) with a zero difference; a month over its limit reports over budget with a positive signed difference; the signed difference equals total minus limit in every case. Tests assert the exact classification and the exact signed integer difference.
