# ISS-0005 - Report monthly totals and per-category breakdown

## Summary

Implement the Reporter's read models over Ledger data: a per-month total of `amountCents` ordered by month ascending, and, for a given month, a breakdown of that total by category ordered by category label ascending. The Reporter reads the Ledger and never mutates it.

## Scope

Build the Reporter's derived read models as pure-JS functions under `src/` that read expenses from the Ledger (via the ISS-0002 reads) and never mutate it.

Monthly totals: group expenses by calendar month, derived from the `YYYY-MM` prefix of each expense `date`, and for each month present in the Ledger sum `amountCents` across that month's expenses. The result is ordered by month ascending. A month with no expenses does not appear in the result.

Category totals within a month: for a given month, break the monthly total down by category, summing `amountCents` per category. The breakdown is ordered by category label ascending.

## Out Of Scope

Budget evaluation (ISS-0006), the Ledger total (ISS-0003), CSV export (ISS-0007), and CLI wiring (ISS-0008). This issue does not format output for the console.

## Verification

Unit tests in `test/` (node:test) to full branch coverage: monthly totals over expenses spanning multiple months produce one entry per month, each summing that month's `amountCents`, ordered by month ascending; a month with no expenses is absent; an empty Ledger yields an empty monthly result; the category breakdown for a month sums `amountCents` per category and is ordered by category label ascending; a requested month with no expenses yields an empty breakdown; reports do not mutate the Ledger (the stored expenses are unchanged after a report). The deterministic gate `gate:test:monthly-report` must be green (node --test exit 0) before this issue is reported complete.
